Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner in Nutritional Supplements

At Vitz Express, we are a premier supplier of nutritional supplements worldwide, serving businesses across the globe with a commitment to quality and innovation. With over 20 years of market presence, we've established ourselves as a reliable partner for retailers, distributors, and healthcare institutions seeking premium nutritional products.


Our comprehensive range includes vitamins, minerals, protein supplements, herbal extracts, and specialized formulations that meet diverse health needs. We pride ourselves on maintaining rigorous quality control standards throughout our supply chain, ensuring that every product that bears our name upholds the highest standards of purity, potency, and effectiveness.


Our extensive experience in the B2B supplement market has allowed us to build a network of satisfied clients across six continents. We understand the unique challenges of the nutritional supplement industry and work closely with our partners to develop customized supply solutions that help them succeed in a competitive marketplace.
